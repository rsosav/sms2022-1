Supernatural Ministry School 2021
We strongly recommend that anyone who is considered to be “high-risk” under CDC guidelines, remain at home. All attendees must agree that they will not attend the event if any one or more of the following is true prior to event days:
  • Within the 14 days prior, they have tested positive or have been exposed to someone who has tested positive for COVID-19.
  • Within the 48 hours prior, they have experienced symptoms of COVID-19. (e.g., a fever of 100.4⁰F or higher, cough, shortness of breath or difficulty breathing, chills, repeated shaking, muscle pain/ache, headache, sore throat, loss of taste or smell, nasal congestion, runny nose, vomiting, diarrhea, fatigue or any other symptoms associated with COVID-19 identified by the CDC).
CDC Guidelines:
The safety of our guests, employees and volunteers remain our top priority. Please read the following guidelines as we work to provide a clean and safe experience for everyone:
  • Face masks required at all times (inside and outside the building), other than when actively eating or drinking. Face mask must fully cover the nose and mouth.
  • Please follow social distancing guidelines and do not form groups or congregate in the lobby or any other indoor areas.
  • Always follow the directions from church staff.
  • Enter only through the designated doors and do not open any doors.
  • Follow all posted guidelines for use of restrooms.
  • Family members may sit together, while maintaining social distance from other attendees.
  • Please do not rearrange any chairs or seating formations.
  • At the end of the service, please promptly exit the building through the designated doors. Do not linger in the temple, lobby or parking lot.
  • Please maintain social distancing outside the building as well.
  • ADD: Wash hands thoroughly and use hand sanitizing stations throughout the building.
** Please note that our team conducts thorough disinfection protocols before and after each service and during the event.
What are the changes to SMS?
This school is a multi-track experience into the fivefold and more.The first two days of this school (Tuesday & Wednesday) will consist of 7 tracks. You will have the option to select the track you want to commit to for those two days at the time of registration.
Where will the tracks be held?
All tracks will take place in different locations. Some locations will require transportation, so no need to worry about driving. Charter buses will be provided to take the registrant to and from their track location.
Can I select multiple tracks for one registrant?
This school curriculum has been curated to teach, train, empower and mobilize. Therefore, in order for the registrant to be fully mobilized in the selected track, they must attend the same track both days.
Can I change the track I originally selected?
Each track location has been fitted to the COVID capacity. Therefore, we will not be able to accommodate last minute changes.
